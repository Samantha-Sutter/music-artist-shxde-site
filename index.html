ARTIST | PRODUCER | DJ | CYBERPUNK

Founded in 2017 and hailing from Milwaukee Wisconsin, shxde’s distinct sound and visual aesthetic has come to be synonymous with all things cyberpunk. Taking heavy influence from Trance/Electronica/Trap and Dubstep shxde has cultivated and helped pioneer the sub genre known as ‘Hardwave.’

shxde not only pushes boundaries sonically, he also specializes in Graphic Design and Artist Branding. With over 100 satisfied clients shxde has personally crafted the visual identity of many talented artists, designed and branded live events, URL shows and much more.

In 2021 shxde began the rollout of his anticipated experimental album ‘x’ with three singles: Voices, Echo and Maze.

The full length album will be available to stream on all platforms December 10th 2021.
